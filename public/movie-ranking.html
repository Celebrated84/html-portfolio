The Best Movies According to Cedric

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar


The best of the Christopher Nolan movies. A former-NASA pilot leads a crew to the stars in hopes of finding a new home for the people left on Earth.

Goodfellas

A hard-hitting crime drama from famed director Martin Scorcese.

Malcolm X

The best of Denzel Washington, from director Spike Lee. He portays civil rights activist and black movement leader Malcolm X.
